# Code of Conduct

## Purpose

This repository is maintained as a professional technical portfolio documenting laboratory science, industrial microbiology, quality systems, public health, and data analytics.

The objective is to encourage respectful, evidence-based technical discussion.

---

## Expected Behaviour

Participants are expected to:

- Communicate professionally and respectfully.
- Support technical statements with evidence where appropriate.
- Respect intellectual property and attribution.
- Encourage constructive discussion and continuous learning.
- Maintain scientific integrity.

---

## Unacceptable Behaviour

The following will not be tolerated:

- Personal attacks or harassment.
- Discrimination of any kind.
- Spam or promotional content unrelated to the repository.
- Misrepresentation of data or scientific findings.
- Publication of confidential or proprietary information.

---

## Enforcement

The repository owner reserves the right to remove inappropriate comments, issues, or other interactions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

---

## Contact

Questions regarding this Code of Conduct may be directed through GitHub Issues or the contact information provided in the repository README.
